## The decision timeline (how to read this folder)

The ideas were committed **once**, on paper, before a single simulation — then the sims reshaped the details, one
phase at a time. The spine held; specific mechanisms were overturned, mostly in the author's favour. The lineage:

```
ideas1.md   ─►   Phases 1–6      ─►   gd-replan/        ─►   Phases 7–10
(origin          (Stage 1: the        (the depth-readout      (Stage 2: the
 blueprint,       SCFF cheap           research hinge that      GD "namer")
 pre-sim)         brain)               opened the GD era)
```

- **[`ideas1.md`](ideas1.md)** — the **origin blueprint**, told as a story: the two-brain 80/20, *"direction is the
  expensive part of learning — pay for it once,"* the nine-chapter plan (SCFF · mono-forward dual-rail · the LUT ·
  read-don't-write taps · the plasticity gradient · the threshold gate · sleep · residual boosting blocks), and the
  experiment ladder. Written **before Phase 1**. Its last line: *"the shape of the answer is committed; the numbers
  are what we're about to go find."* The phases kept the spine and overturned much of the rest — the-essence2
  **Part II §E** narrates exactly what survived.
- **[`main.ideas.v1.md`](main.ideas.v1.md)** — the **running decision record**: the three approved calls (N1–N3) +
  the supporting structure and every phase's close-out delta (S1–S14). The live ledger of what we committed and
  *why*, one line each, updated as each phase reported. *(The "v1" is historical — there is only one set.)*
- **[`phase4-opinion.md`](phase4-opinion.md)** — the author's **prescient pre-research reflection** (~Phase 4): the
  intuition that seeded the GD era — *read-don't-write*, the near-identity residual, the "lazy depth" decider, the
  "danger → learn hard" effort gate, the hippocampus loop. Written **before** the namer was researched; the phases
  later confirmed several of these by name.
- **[`phase4-problem.md`](phase4-problem.md)** — the **depth-decay problem statement** (the warm-up before the
  research session): what breaks, where the data says it breaks, and why — the diagnosis that it is a **direction**
  failure (the representation drifts off the class manifold past ~L5), reconciled against the author's own reflection.
- **[`gd-replan/`](gd-replan/README.md)** — the **depth-readout research hinge** that opened Stage 2: the map, the
  literature, and the re-plan. Front door: [`gd-replan/README.md`](gd-replan/README.md); the actionable ladder:
  [`gd-replan/replan.md`](gd-replan/replan.md); the authoritative post-review decisions:
  [`gd-replan/review-and-revisions.md`](gd-replan/review-and-revisions.md). The spine of every change: *preserve /
  read the class **direction**, never a magnitude.*
