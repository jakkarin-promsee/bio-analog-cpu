## 1. The five strategic reframes (how the sweep repositions the project)

1. **The closed-form namer is commodity; the value lives in the BULK + the SUBSTRATE.** A prototype/closed-form head on frozen features is a solved, widely-published shape (Janson, SimpleCIL, FSA, F-OAL). SLDA/RanPAC is *validated, not novel*. Stop pitching the head. *(t1.5, t1.2)*

2. **The core bet is SUPPORTED — the most-backed claim in the sweep.** A label-free, self-grown frozen representation *is* a good continual-learning substrate; SSL backbones forget less and transfer OOD, biggest at small data = our regime (Gallardo is nearly our exact machine). *(t1.7)*

3. **Our capability SHAPE ("wins continual/noise, trails static") is a family LAW, not a defect.** It's the published **locality tax** of every non-exact-gradient rule (Bartunov, Refinetti) — and our flat-vector substrate sits on the *good* side of the conditioning line that kills DFA on conv. P4/P10/P11 are lawful. *(t1.6)*

4. **The energy win must pin to the ANALOG SUBSTRATE, never to forward-only-ness — and that is the *defensible* story.** Forward-only ≠ energy-efficient (Spyra); the honest matched-accuracy protocol (report the algorithm-joules even though they lose, substrate factor as a separate multiplier) is exactly our P10 R1 discipline (Dehghani, Horowitz, Gholami). The memory wall is the mechanism behind P11's *growing* substrate factor. *(t1.1, t1.3, t4.5)*

5. **"The substrate's physics IS the math" — confirmed from six independent hardware/physics literatures.** Scap = the field's best update device; memristor decay = the SSM `A` matrix; LTC = an RC element; CAM recall = a physical settle; EqProp = local physics at equilibrium; eligibility trace = a leaky cap; and a ridge solve = the equilibrium of an RLC network. The founding conceit is no longer a slogan. *(Tier 4, t5.1)*

## 4. The one open research problem the whole horizon reduces to

**A gradient-free / closed-form learning-and-routing rule for the recurrent parts.** Memory (delta-rule), recall
(crossbar), settle (physics), self-signal (noise) all have substrate-native gradient-free forms. The **recurrent
weights' learning** (EqProp needs a nudge phase; SSM decay & delta-gates are BPTT-trained today) and the **coordinator's
routing** (every published win credits *learned* routing) do not. Solve this once and the last non-native pieces of the
north star become native. **This is the flagship open problem** — Tier 3 and Tier 4 converge on it independently.

## 5. The honest limits (kept, not buried)

- **The head is commodity** — novelty must be argued from the bulk + substrate, never the namer. *(t1.5)*
- **The P11 autocorrelated-stream floor is INTRINSIC to stream-grown SSL, not a substrate bug** — and superposition-under-temporal-correlation is its mechanism. Reframe as a known field limit. *(t1.7, t2.5, t5.2)*
- **Compression was never measured** — the spare-capacity slack could be dead memorization (forward-only lacks grokking's cleanup pressure). *(t5.2)*
- **On-array update is where analog learning historically dies** — SCFF's behavior under it is unmeasured; the biggest realism risk. *(t4.1)*
- **CLP-SNN on Loihi 2 (Intel 2025)** is a *published, on-silicon* competitor on our exact pitch — engage it, don't ignore it. *(t4.3)*
- **Two committed P8/P9 decisions are re-openable** as separate future experiments: the parked tap-drift direction gate (parked for a non-reason) and grid-4 cadence (possibly conservative). *(t2.4, t2.3)*
