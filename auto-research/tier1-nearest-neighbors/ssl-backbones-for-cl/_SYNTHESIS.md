# Synthesis — SSL / label-free frozen backbones for continual learning  (Tier 1, t1.7)
**The question:** Is a label-free, self-grown frozen representation a good continual-learning substrate — i.e. does the SSL-backbone-for-CL literature SUPPORT or UNDERCUT our founding bet?
**Already in `draft6.0/research/`:** the curated library carries the *objective* (`survey/SCFF.detail.md`, `papers/phase1-2/scff.md`) and one SSL exemplar (`papers/phase1-2/byol.md`), plus the continual-learning north-star note (`north-star/5-continual.md`) and the Phase-5 continual-home reframe. It does **not** carry the continual-*SSL* literature — the empirical case for/against "label-free representations forget less." That is exactly this topic's contribution; nothing below duplicates the curated set.

## The landscape (2–4 paragraphs)
This is the literature that stress-tests our single most load-bearing assumption: that you can grow a representation **without labels** and get continual robustness partly for free. It splits into three camps.

**Camp 1 — the case FOR label-free (the founding-bet evidence).** LUMP (Madaan 2022, ICLR oral) is the cleanest statement: run the same split-task protocol with a supervised vs an SSL loss, probe both, and the SSL representation forgets less, transfers better OOD, and sits in visibly flatter loss basins — labels are the poison because they drag features onto *this task's* decision boundaries. Cossu 2024 (Neural Networks) formalizes "continual pre-training" and runs a factor decomposition (modality × architecture × protocol): the *protocol* (SSL vs supervised) is the dominant factor for forgetting, and SSL retains best **with no CL strategy at all** — no replay, no distillation, no regularizer. Gallardo 2021 (BMVC) is the nearest twin to our actual machine: an SSL backbone feeding **Deep SLDA** and other online heads, where SSL beats supervised pretraining across three heads, with the advantage *growing as pretraining data shrinks* (up to +89% relative at 10-class pretrain). Ostapenko 2022 (CoLLAs) sweeps a zoo of frozen encoders and finds SSL is the better substrate specifically **out-of-distribution** — and our stream is always OOD (there is no pretrain; everything is unseen until seen). Davari 2022 (CVPR) supplies the measurement instrument: an optimal-linear-probe defines *representation* forgetting, which turns out far milder than classifier forgetting — the representation drifts benignly while the readout goes stale. That is our bulk/namer split, published.

**Camp 2 — the skeptics and the ceiling (the undercut).** Marczak 2024 (ECCV) is the sharpest: give the *supervised* model the same MLP projector head SSL always carries, and supervised-with-projector **beats** SSL at continual representation learning. The transfer magic attributed to label-freeness may actually live in the projector — a buffer layer that absorbs objective-specific distortion so the backbone stays general. Every "SSL forgets less" result (LUMP, Davari-style) becomes an under-controlled comparison. Meanwhile CaSSLe (Fini 2022, CVPR) shows that plain sequential SSL, unaided, *does* catastrophically degrade at scale — label-free is not sufficient alone — and needs a distillation anchor to hold. Purushwalkam 2022 (ECCV) audits our exact regime (infinite, temporally-correlated, drifting stream) and shows conventional SSL breaks on all three counts; the temporal-correlation collapse is precisely our P11 autocorrelated-stream FLOOR, and the paper's verdict is that it is **intrinsic to stream-grown SSL**, not an artifact of any one substrate.

**Camp 3 — the architecture echoes.** POCON (Gomez-Villa 2024, WACV) reproduces our two-timescale (fast plastic expert / slow consolidated main) instinct *inside* the unsupervised representation, with distillation as the consolidation channel, and warns that a single unaided SSL network under-adapts or forgets on long task sequences. Osiris (Zhang 2024, CoLLAs oral) argues UCL needs three separated objectives — plasticity, stability, and **cross-task consolidation** — and reports that *semantically structured* streams make continual learners do **better** than shuffled ones: our "coherent drift is home turf" (gas-sensor win) result, arriving in the SSL community.

## How WE differ  ← the money section
**Verdict: the core bet is broadly SUPPORTED — this is the single most-supported claim in the whole tier-1 sweep — but with two live caveats we have not closed, and every one of these backbones is offline in ways we are not.**

Supported, honestly: five of ten cards (LUMP, Gallardo, Cossu, Ostapenko, Davari) independently converge that a label-free representation is a good CL substrate, and Gallardo is nearly our exact operating point (label-free backbone + closed-form streaming LDA head). Our two-organ architecture — benign representation drift + a re-anchoring readout — is not a lucky guess; Davari names it as the field's own diagnosis and our P9.4 proto-reanchor is his prototype construction in different clothes.

But the support is **not** a blank cheque, and the honest placement has two threats. (1) **Attribution (Marczak).** The "label-free *causes* robustness" story is confounded: the win may be the contrastive-objective *architecture* — and our InfoNCE + w=2 coordination window is plausibly a "projector-equivalent" absorbing objective distortion away from the tapped features. We must **not** lean public claims on label-freeness *causing* continual robustness until we run the control. (2) **The floor is intrinsic (Purushwalkam + CaSSLe + POCON).** Unaided single-network stream-grown SSL degrades at scale and starves on autocorrelated streams — this is our P11 floor, and the literature says it is a property of the regime, not a bug we can tune out of the SCFF cell.

What stays **uniquely ours**, unmatched by any card here: every one of these backbones is grown **offline, IID(-within-chunk), multi-epoch, by backprop**, then frozen (or backprop-distilled at task boundaries), with a **raw-image replay buffer**. Ours is grown **forward-only, local, one-pass, on the deployment stream itself, with no task boundaries, no raw-data replay through the objective, and bound to an analog cost model** — and it *keeps* learning (rotating) for life rather than freezing after pretrain. None of them ask whether the backbone could keep learning label-free *during deployment*, which is our entire premise. The overlap is the bet; the substrate is ours alone.

## The gap / what we haven't tried
- **The Marczak attribution control (highest-value).** A supervised-contrast SCFF variant — labels define the InfoNCE positives, still local, still forward-only — vs our label-free cell, everything else locked, measured on BWT/retention. If supervised-contrast *matches* label-free, robustness credits the *objective structure*, not the absence of labels — which actually **strengthens** the chip story (labels are absent for cost, not for magic). One arm, one variable.
- **MinRed / max-spread LUT-negative eviction** (Purushwalkam) — a closed-form-cheap diversity rule for the LUT negatives that directly targets our named P11 autocorrelated-stream floor. Testable on HAR/ELEC2 with one knob.
- **Optimal-probe forgetting curve** (Davari) for the SCFF bulk, reported alongside our prequential numbers — the field's accepted instrument, would let us cite-compare our "rotates-not-forgets" claim.
- **The "how much pretraining does the bulk need" sweep** (Gallardo's 10/100-class small-data finding) — freeze the bulk at several stream positions, measure namer accuracy vs stream-position; our small, stream-grown regime is exactly where SSL's advantage is documented largest.
- **Split contrast into within-recent (plasticity) vs LUT-past (consolidation) negatives** with separate weights (Osiris) — make our accidental consolidation space deliberate; one knob on the P11 arenas.
- **Loss-landscape flatness probe** (LUMP) as an internal invariant on the SCFF objective across the stream.

## Leads spawned
- **Supervised-contrastive / SupCon-style local rules** as the label-injected control arm (the Marczak control) → candidate topic.
- **Projector / feature-buffer literature** — why throwing away the head aids transfer; whether our w=2 window is a projector-equivalent.
- **MinRed / minimum-redundancy replay-buffer mechanics** → a max-spread LUT-negative diversity rule.
- **Structured / environment-coherent stream benchmarks** (Osiris, egocentric video; Purushwalkam embodied streams) → real-stream arenas beyond P11's eight.
- **Continual pre-training at LLM scale** (arXiv 2403.08763) + "Future of Continual Learning in the Era of Foundation Models" (arXiv 2506.03320) — the scaling side of the same claim.
- **Optimal-probe / CKA feature-similarity drift measures** as cheap online probes of benign vs destructive drift.
